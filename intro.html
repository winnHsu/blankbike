A.
Executive Summary

This page introduces the ____Bike Program, an exclusive, single-brand naming rights opportunity for a fleet of approximately 6,000 e-bikes operating across Manhattan. The program is designed as a long-term, city-scale platform that combines high-density mobile OOH with a useful, everyday mobility service for New Yorkers and visitors.

At its core, the ____Bike asset operates as a dual-purpose platform: both an always-on media network of thousands of branded e-bikes circulating throughout the nation’s leading media market, and a consumer-facing utility encountered daily by riders and pedestrians alike. Building on the proven effectiveness of bike-share naming rights as a marketing model, the ____Bike Program represents a next-generation execution focused on a fully e-bike fleet, a Manhattan-first footprint, and a high level of creative and experiential control for a single, category-leading partner.

The Math, Data, and Omnipresence
The Math, Data, and Omnipresence

With data showing an average riding time of 9.1 hours per bike daily, a single bike, whether parked in front of a residential building or in transit on a key artery like 5th Avenue, generates a baseline of 2,730 daily high-quality (conservative estimation of visible radius & view shed) impressions. For 6,000 bikes, that's 16.38 million repetitive impressions every single day.

⦿  High-Density Daily Circulation: Our rented bikes average 109 miles of movement per unit per day, creating a level of on-street circulation that drastically amplifies real-world visibility, repeat exposures, and total impression delivery across Manhattan’s most valuable corridors.

⦿  100% E-Bike Fleet: E-bikes see 2-3x more use and travel 3-4x the distance of pedal bikes. This "supercharges" the impressions, reach, and frequency per asset.

⦿  Manhattan-First Focus: Over 80% of our 70 docking stations are located in Manhattan, ensuring concentrated, not diluted, impressions. With 4 major hubs in West Village, East Village, Hudson Yards, and Upper Manhattan, the network spans the island end to end, delivering high-frequency visibility to New York’s most valuable audiences: finance and B2B decision-makers, high-spend tourists, and affluent residents.
