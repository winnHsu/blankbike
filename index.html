The Vision

The ____Bike Program gives one brand the opportunity to become part of New York City’s cultural and physical landscape — not through traditional billboards, but through the city’s own movement.

By branding a fleet of 3,000+ professional e-bikes across Manhattan, your brand becomes woven into New York’s daily rhythm: the streets, the commutes, the rides, and the everyday lives of millions.
